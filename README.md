Campaign Performance Intelligence
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard solution for analyzing advertising performance across Google Ads, Meta Ads, and TikTok Ads platforms with data cleaning using Python.

🎯 Objective \
This project addresses the critical challenge of optimizing digital marketing budgets by providing unified visibility into campaign performance across multiple platforms, enabling data-driven budget allocation decisions. 

📊 Dashboard Overview \
Page 1: Campaign Optimization Dashboard \
Executive KPI Cards: Tracking of ad spend, revenue, and ROAS \
Platform Comparison: Side-by-side analysis of Google Ads, Meta Ads, and TikTok Ads performance \
Campaign Analysis: CPC vs. CTR comparisons by campaign type and ROAS by industry \
Interactive Filtering: Drill down capabilities by platform, campaign type, and industry segment

Page 2: Executive Performance Dashboard \
Segment Efficiency Analysis: Four-quadrant framework (High/Low Spend vs. High/Low ROAS) \
Strategic Performance Evaluation: Cross-platform, industry, and campaign type combinations \
Trend Analysis: Ad spend versus revenue over time with spend share by platform \
Budget Optimization Insights: Identification of high-opportunity and underperforming segments

🔍 Key Metrics Tracked \
Ad Spend: Total advertising investment across all platforms \
Revenue: Total revenue generated from advertising campaigns \
ROAS: Return on Ad Spend - primary efficiency metric \
Impressions & Clicks: Volume-based metrics for reach and engagement context

💡 Business Benefits \
Unified Visibility: Consolidates fragmented marketing data from multiple platforms \
Segment-Level Clarity: Enables comparison across industries, platforms, and campaign types \
Efficiency-Driven Decisions: ROAS-focused analysis highlights value generation and optimization opportunities \
Strategic Budget Allocation: Data-backed insights guide investment toward high-performing segments

🔄 Analysis Framework \
The dashboard employs a four-category efficiency framework:

High Spend & High ROAS: Scale opportunities \
High Spend & Low ROAS: Optimization candidates \
Low Spend & High ROAS: High-potential segments \
Low Spend & Low ROAS: Monitor/pause candidates

📋 Usage Workflow \
Baseline Review: Establish overall performance KPIs \
Cross-Segment Comparison: Identify top and underperforming segments \
Inefficiency Flagging: Detect high-spend/low-return scenarios \
Budget Reallocation: Optimize toward higher-performing segments

🚀 Getting Started \
This dashboard solution is designed for marketing leadership and analytics teams seeking to transform advertising performance from fragmented metrics into strategic decision intelligence.
